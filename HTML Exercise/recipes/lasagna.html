Lasagna

This is the yummiest lasagna ever. Lazy orange cats love it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
